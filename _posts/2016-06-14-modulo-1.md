# Your first code

Let's make a pretty map.

<pre>
library(maps)
map('world')
</pre>

Let's make a map of Mozambique

<pre>
map('world', 'moz')
</pre>

Let's make a prettier map of Mozambique

<pre>
addMarkers(addTiles(leaflet()),
  lng=32.808352, lat=-25.407195, popup="Here we are")
</pre>
